KNOW ABOUT US

First ever web-based health care project of STEM PTECH Students at Taguig City University

We have service-software for medical facilities and continuous development and quick implementation to deliver our software as
a services. Expertise in healthcare, statistical accuracy, and cutting-edge technology are all combined in every upgraded version.
This website will also advance the improvement of facilities for health care for people in Taguig, it will provide easy access and a
quality medication samples provided by our program, enabling them to research and develop therapies for a variety of conditions.

THE HISTORY

The research team of STEM PTECH 12-A at Taguig City University has six members: Tolentino, Tomas, Salazar, Tortogo, Trapane, and Perez.
In the capstone research subject, this project has been accepted by Professor Jeric T. Nuez as their research study for the end of the final
semester. This web-based version was created with the code editor Brackets, free and open-source software licensed under the MIT License.
The researchers used a tool called HTML, which is the standard markup language for documents designed to be displayed in a web browser.
Next is CSS, a simple mechanism for adding style like fonts, colors, and spacing to Web documents. Lastly is JavaScript, which is a scripting
language that enables you to create dynamically updating content, control multimedia, animate images, and pretty much everything else,
they used these tools to complete the whole operation of a program.
